Nutrition for Workout Enthusiasts

Welcome to your guide for fueling your body to support your workout goals! Proper nutrition is key to maximizing performance, recovery, and muscle growth.

Key Nutrition Tips

  • Protein: Aim for 1.6-2.2g per kg of body weight daily to support muscle repair (e.g., chicken, eggs, tofu).
  • Carbohydrates: Fuel your workouts with 4-7g per kg of body weight daily (e.g., oats, rice, sweet potatoes).
  • Fats: Include healthy fats (0.5-1g per kg) for energy and hormone balance (e.g., avocado, nuts, olive oil).
  • Hydration: Drink at least 2-3 liters of water daily, more if you're sweating heavily.
  • Timing: Eat a balanced meal 2-3 hours before exercising and a protein-carb combo within 30 minutes post-workout.
