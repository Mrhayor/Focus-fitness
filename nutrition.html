Eating right is just as important as your workouts. Fuel up with the right nutrients to crush your goals, recover faster, and feel awesome.

Top Nutrition Tips for Fitness

  • Protein Power: Aim for 1.6-2.2g per kg of body weight daily to rebuild muscles. Think chicken, eggs, or plant-based tofu!
  • Carb Charge: Get 4-7g per kg of body weight for energy. Load up on oats, sweet potatoes, or whole grains.
  • Fat Fuel: Include 0.5-1g per kg of healthy fats for stamina and balance—avocado, nuts, or olive oil are your friends.
  • Stay Hydrated: Drink 2-3 liters of water daily (more if you sweat a lot) to keep moving strong.
  • Timing Matters: Eat a balanced meal 2-3 hours pre-workout, and grab a protein-carb snack (like a shake + fruit) within 30 mins post-workout.
